The problem

Most content on business websites was written to fill pages, not to win work. Service descriptions are generic. Blog posts don't connect to the buyer journey. There's no clear hierarchy — everything sits at the same level, competing with itself.

Meanwhile, the businesses that win in search and AI are the ones with deep, structured, purposeful content. Every page serves a role. Every piece connects to the whole. Content compounds rather than clutters.

How we approach it

Structured for machines
