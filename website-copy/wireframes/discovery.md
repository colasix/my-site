### SECTION 2: PROBLEM AGITATION (BUSINESS MAKING DECISIONS BLIND WITHOUT DATA)

**[H2]** Why clarity costs less than guessing.

**[AGITATION NARRATIVE — 150-200 words]**

Most business owners make decisions about digital investment without a baseline. They've noticed traffic softening, or they suspect their website isn't working as hard as their business does, but they don't know *why* or *what to change*. They're caught between the pressure to act and the risk of wasting more money on the wrong solution.

Guessing leads to expensive mistakes: rebuilding when a positioning refresh would suffice. Investing in content strategies that don't align with how the market actually searches. Betting on platform changes that don't solve the real problem. Every wrong guess delays the business's competitive position and compounds the cost of the eventual correction.

Discovery changes this. Instead of making decisions blind, you get specific, evidence-based recommendations grounded in data: how your customers search, where you're currently visible and invisible, how your positioning compares to competitors, and what actually needs to change.
