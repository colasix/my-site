# WebXC Wireframe Phases & Progress Tracker

Based on the site architecture defined in the sitemap, this document tracks progress through four distinct phases. Each phase builds on the previous, with global components (header, footer) deployed first, then Tier 1 (core entry), then Tier 2 (trust/navigation), then Tier 3 (service detail and secondary conversion).

**Status Key:**
- `[ ]` — Not started
- `[~]` — In progress
- `[x]` — Complete
- `[R]` — Needs revision

## Notes & Assumptions

- **Phase 1** should be completed before any tier-specific pages begin. This ensures consistency across the site.
- **Phase 2** should be completed early to establish the entry experience and persona routing logic.
- **Phase 3** pages can proceed in parallel. All should be reviewed together for consistency.
- **Phase 4** depends on Phase 1 and should follow Phase 2. Service pages link to each other as alternatives and to Ongoing as evolution.
- **Phase 5** can proceed in parallel with Phases 3-4 but requires content strategy input on specific topics.
