# Messaging Reference — WebXC

## Core Message

WebXC makes established businesses clearly understood, trusted, and citable across search and AI, by leading with business strategy, building what you own, and structuring your digital presence so the machines that now influence your customers can actually find you, interpret you, and recommend you.

## One-Liner

WebXC is a principal-led digital performance partner for established New Zealand businesses, turning real-world expertise into a website that converts visitors into customers and is structured to be discovered and cited by both search engines and AI.

## CTA

**Primary:** "Let's Have a Conversation"
**Secondary:** "Take the Self-Assessment" (workshop)
**Discovery:** "Book a Strategic Review"

## Persona Messaging

### The Legacy Moderniser
- Situation: Real-world reputation far outpaces digital presence
- Pain: Dated site, losing enquiries to first impressions
- Message: "Your expertise deserves a digital presence that proves it"

### The Burned Decision Maker
- Situation: Paid agencies, got overcharged and under-delivered
- Pain: Junior staff, no accountability, nothing stuck
- Message: "Principal-led. Everything owned by you. No handoff."

### The Strategically Unaware Leader
- Situation: Traffic softening, uncertain why
- Pain: Invisible to AI, competitors occupying their space
- Message: "The way customers find businesses has structurally changed"

### The Ethical Medical Specialist
- Situation: Reputation is the product, zero tolerance for risk
- Pain: Needs accuracy and authority without aggressive tactics
- Message: "Accuracy, authority, and safety built into the foundation"

## Top Three Benefits

1. **Your website converts like your best salesperson.** Conversion-engineered architecture: 90+ PageSpeed, sales-logic layouts, trust architecture, ICP-based navigation.

2. **Discoverable and citable by AI, not just Google.** Dual-channel semantic architecture: schema markup, entity resolution, structured data. One investment serves both search and AI.

3. **Every dollar builds an asset you permanently own.** No opaque retainers. Strategy, structure, content, and code all belong to the client. Nothing disappears when a contract ends.

## Proof Points

- Over 65% of searches end without a click
- AI Overviews can halve traffic to the top organic result
- 73% of B2B websites lost significant traffic between 2024–2025
- Brands cited in AI responses earn ~35% more organic clicks

## Language Rules (summary)

- Short declarative sentences, average under 15 words
- No em dashes (zero tolerance)
- NZ spelling: optimise, programme, colour, centre
- No colons in headings
- Active voice exclusively
- No hedging: "we build" not "we help you build"
- No fluff: no "leverage," "synergy," "holistic," "seamless," "cutting-edge"
- Contractions standard: we're, you'll, it's, don't
- Second person for reader, first person plural for WebXC
- Full rules in `/writing-style.md`
