## Entry Point: Conversation → Discovery

### Step 1: Initial Conversation (Free)

Exploratory discussion to understand the business, current digital presence, and what's prompting the enquiry. Fit conversation, not a pitch. For workshop attendees, builds on the self-assessment they've already completed.

**Client-facing name:** "Let's have a conversation" / "initial consultation"

### Step 2: Discovery & Strategic Recommendations (Paid)

Structured discovery session producing strategic and tactical recommendations:

- Business positioning and messaging review
- Current website assessment (structure, content depth, semantic signals, technical readiness)
- AI visibility check (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Claude)
- Competitor comparison (digital presence in search and AI)
- Search intent mapping (visibility across the buyer journey)
- Entity signal assessment (how clearly the business is defined as a distinct entity)
- Prioritised strategic and tactical recommendations

**What the client receives:** Written report with clear findings and recommended path forward (Option A, Option B, or specific actions for internal team).

**Why it's paid:** Requires principal-level thinking, not templated auditing. Client gets something actionable even if engagement doesn't progress.

**Client-facing name:** "Discovery & Strategic Review"

## Option A: Positioning, Content & Search Optimisation

**For:** Businesses whose existing website structure is adequate but whose positioning, content, and digital signals don't reflect the strength of the business.

**Client-facing name:** Working title — "Positioning & Content Optimisation"

### What we deliver:

**Business Positioning & Messaging**
- Clarified market positioning
- Commercial messaging framework (headlines, value propositions, proof points)
- Call-to-action strategy and conversion logic review

**Product/Service Taxonomy & Content Architecture**
- Service and product taxonomy (clear hierarchy by customer need, not internal org chart)
- Content audit (thin/generic/redundant pages for consolidation or removal)
- Search intent alignment across the funnel (awareness, consideration, decision)
- Content development and restructuring (dual-layer: persuasive for humans, structured for AI)
- FAQ and question-based content (structured for AI extraction)
- E-E-A-T signal implementation (credentials, case studies, evidence)

**Personal Brand & Entity Alignment**
- Personal brand / persona development (key people as distinct credible entities)
- Business entity resolution (consistent definition across website, directories, structured data)
- Entity relationship mapping (services ↔ people ↔ credentials ↔ locations ↔ proof)

**On-Site Technical & Semantic Improvements**
- Schema markup (Organisation, Person, Service, FAQ, Review, HowTo)
- Internal linking and page structure improvements
- AI crawler accessibility review and fixes
- Metadata, heading structure, on-page semantic optimisation

**Off-Site Recommendations**
- Review strategy (which platforms, how to manage reviews)
- Citation and directory recommendations
- Earned mention opportunities (PR, industry bodies, partnerships)
- Social presence recommendations

**What the client ends up with:** Digital presence where positioning is clear, content demonstrates genuine expertise, technical signals are calibrated for AI and search, and the business is consistently defined as a distinct credible entity. All owned by the client.

## Option B: Full Strategic Website Build

**For:** Businesses that need a new digital foundation — current site is structurally inadequate, visually dated, technically limiting, or fundamentally misaligned.

**Client-facing name:** Working title — "Full Digital Foundation Build"

### Everything in Option A, plus:

**Commercial Wireframing & UX Design**
- Sales-logic page layouts
- Conversion pathway design
- Trust architecture (strategic placement of proof elements)
- ICP-based navigation (routing visitor types to relevant deep-dive content)

**Visual Design & Component System**
- High-fidelity UI design (established authority, not startup)
- Design system with reusable content blocks
- Custom iconography where needed

**Technical Build & GEO Implementation**
- CMS implementation (WordPress, modular content management)
- Full semantic schema markup
- Core Web Vitals optimisation (90+ scores)
- Security implementation
- AI crawler optimisation
- LLMs.txt and AI-specific access configuration

**Launch, Handover & Training**
- Pre-launch QA
- Team training (CMS, content editing, SEO hygiene, structured data maintenance)
- Content guidelines and style documentation
- Deployment and DNS transition

**What the client ends up with:** Permanent, commercially calibrated digital asset — technically structured for both search and generative AI, with content reflecting genuine business positioning, and a platform the client fully owns and can independently manage.

## Add-On: Ongoing Content & Optimisation Support

**For:** Businesses that want continued strategic support after the foundation is built.

**Client-facing name:** Working title — "Ongoing Content & Visibility Programme"

### What we deliver:

**Content Strategy & Development**
- Ongoing content planning (what to publish, deepen, retire)
- Content creation and optimisation (dual-layer standard)
- Existing content maintenance (periodic review and refresh)
- Decision-stage and conversion content development

**Authority & Visibility Monitoring**
- AI visibility tracking (periodic checks across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Claude)
- Search performance monitoring
- Entity signal monitoring
- Competitive positioning review

**Strategic Advisory**
- Quarterly strategic review
- Off-site opportunity identification
- Guidance on building toward consensus leadership
- Annual strategic review

### How this differs from a traditional retainer:

Not opaque monthly activity reports. Every piece of content is strategically directed, produced to a standard the client approves, and owned entirely by the client. Focus is on building a compounding content asset. If the client's team develops capability to manage content independently, retainer shifts to pure strategic advisory.

## Unique Attributes (for differentiator content)

1. **Principal-Led Strategic Pre-Work** — Positioning, messaging, taxonomy, CTAs, and commercial logic defined before any build or optimisation. We interrogate the spec first.

2. **Owned Asset Focus** — 100% client-owned. On-site structure, architecture, content, entity signals. No manufactured signals, no dependency creation.

3. **Dual-Channel Semantic Architecture** — Technical and content work designed for both search engines and generative AI simultaneously. Addresses real-time retrieval today and training data absorption for future models.

4. **Personal Brand & Persona Entity Alignment** — Key people defined as distinct credible entities with clear expertise signals, structured data, and consistent cross-platform presence.

5. **Actionable Off-Site Recommendations** — Specific, prioritised guidance without conflict of interest (we don't sell execution).
