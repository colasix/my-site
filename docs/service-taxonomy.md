# Service Taxonomy — WebXC

## Entry Flow

```
Initial Conversation (free)
    ↓
Discovery & Strategic Review (paid)
    ↓
Option A or Option B
    ↓
Optional: Ongoing Authority & Visibility Programme
```

## Initial Conversation (Free)

Exploratory, principal-led discussion. Fit assessment, not sales pitch. Establishes whether WebXC's approach matches the client's situation. For clients from the workshop, builds on their self-assessment.

## Discovery & Strategic Review (Paid)

Principal-level analysis, not checkbox auditing. Produces:

- Business clarity review (how clearly the business communicates across all touchpoints)
- Website assessment (structure, content depth, semantic signals, technical readiness)
- AI visibility baseline (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Claude)
- Competitor comparison (search and AI discovery)
- Search intent mapping (visible and invisible across buyer journey)
- Entity signal assessment (how clearly the business registers as a credible entity)
- Prioritised recommendations (what to fix, build, and in what order)

**Output:** Written report with findings and recommended path (Option A, Option B, or independent actions).

## Option A: Content & Digital Authority Programme

**For:** Businesses whose site is structurally adequate but content underperforms.

**What we do:**
- Clarify business messaging and market position
- Restructure content around customer needs and search intent
- Implement semantic and entity signals for AI and search
- Fix conversion pathways

**Three delivery areas:**
1. Higher conversions (clearer messaging, stronger proof, better pathways)
2. More traffic (content and technical signals for search and AI)
3. Easier content management (structured content the team can maintain)

**Everything permanently owned by the client.**

## Option B: Complete Digital Foundation Build

**For:** Businesses where the platform itself is the constraint.

**Everything in Option A, plus:**
- Full website design and rebuild on WordPress
- Modular CMS with content blocks
- Conversion-engineered UX from the ground up
- 90+ Core Web Vitals performance
- Team training for independent management

**Same three delivery areas, built into the architecture from day one.**

## Ongoing Authority & Visibility Programme (Add-on)

**Available after:** Option A or Option B completion.

**What it delivers:**
- Content strategy and development (search intent, AI trends, client expertise)
- Authority and visibility monitoring (AI tracking, search performance, entity signals)
- Strategic advisory (quarterly reviews, off-site opportunities, annual landscape review)

**Principle:** Build a compounding asset, not volume to justify a fee. Shifts to pure advisory if the client's team develops capability.

## Three Outcome Themes (for homepage and marketing)

1. **We increase your conversions** — Commercial messaging, trust architecture, sales-logic layouts, 90+ PageSpeed, mobile parity
2. **We increase your traffic** — Search intent alignment, entity signals, semantic structure, AI crawler optimisation, dual-channel visibility
3. **We make content easier to manage** — Modular CMS, reusable patterns, design separated from content, team training
